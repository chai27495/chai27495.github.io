Deloitte Design Jam

Summary

Designed the prototype of an iPad application to aid the Manager to handle the demand spikes at Zingerman during busy weekends.

Problem

Zingerman faces lot of customers making it really difficult to handle the extreme surge of customers on the football weekends

Goal

Provide a solution on iPad to make the manager more organized in order to shorten the wait and lead to smooth functioning

Team

7

Brainstorming Acitivty

Brainstormed to find out the major factors leading to the problem.

'ZINGAME'

After understanding the main factors we came up with a solution that was addressing the factors found during the Brainstorming session.
