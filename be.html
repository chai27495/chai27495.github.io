UX Research and Design Project

Summary
Role: UX Researcher and Designer
Time frame: 1 year
Tools: Android Studio, SPSS
Methods: Brainstorming, Sketching, User Centered Design, User Research, Participatory Design, Data Collection, Data Analysis

Proved the influence of emotions on keystroke patterns of a person by using User Experience Research and Design

Goal

Prove the influence of emotion on keystroke patterns in Android platform using Auditory Stimuli

Team

Research Process

  • Literature Survey
  • Performed Literature Survey to know about the current research in Keystroke Dynamics and different methods used to prove influence of emotions on Keystroke patterns
  • Implementation
  • Created the plan we were going to follow to prove the influence of emotions
  • Requested IADS sounds and SAM ratings from University of Florida which were to be used for our research
  • Built an Android app using 'Participatory Design' that could record keystroke patterns from users
  • Recorded keystroke patterns from 23 users for different emotions induced through IADS sounds required for analysis
  • Made use of two-way ANOVA in SPSS to prove the influence of emotions on keystroke patterns
  • Wrote a Research paper published by International Journal of Computer Applications
