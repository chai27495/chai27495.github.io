About me

Hi, I am Chaitrali. I am currently pursuing Masters degree in User Experience Design and Research at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My interest in art and love for problem solving through Information Technology Engineering brought me to this amazing field of User Experience Design and Research.

I am very fueled by my passion of balancing my technical and creative skills to come up with innovative solutions to solve users' problems. I am currently balancing these skills as a Web Migration Analyst Intern to redesign the Michigan Flora website. I want to bring a change in the world and am up for any challenge that comes my way.

Currently I am seeking for Summer Internship opportunities where I can challenge myself and utilize the best of my abilities.
