About me

Hi, I am Chaitrali. I am an aspiring User Experience Designer and Researcher. My passion for art and interest in problem solving through Information Technology Engineering brought me to this amazing field of User Experience Design and Research
.
I am very fueled by my passion for balancing my technical and creative skills to come up with innovative design solutions to solve users' problems. Currently, I am balancing these skills by pursuing Masters in User Experience Design and Research at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. I have worked as a Web Migration Analyst Intern where I provided recommendations to redesign the Michigan Flora website by performing User Research and created prototypes using Sketch to support my recommendation. I have also worked as a Graduate Research Assistant at Institute for Research on Innovation and Science . I want to bring a change in the world of User Experience by innovating new techniques and am up for any challenge that comes my way.

Besides academics, I am a big time foodie exploring new restaurants all the time. Read my food blog here.
